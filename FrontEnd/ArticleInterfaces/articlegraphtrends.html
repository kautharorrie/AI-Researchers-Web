This graph tracks the growth rate of the AI researcher community by displaying the number of new ratings awarded per year. C rated researchers have displayed the most consistent growth along with B and Y, whereas very few researchers have been given the title of A and P.

This graph tracks the total number of new AI researchers in South Africa per year from 2017 to 2022. The size of the community has increased consistently over time with a slight deviation from the trend in 2022.
